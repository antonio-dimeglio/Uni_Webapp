DisGeNET Covid Database

DisGeNET is a discovery platform containing one of the largest publicly available collections of genes and variants associated to human diseases. This is a very versatile platform that can be used for different research purposes including the analysis of the properties of disease genes, the validation of computationally predicted disease genes and the evaluation of text-mining methods performance.

DisGeNET has also a COVID-19 data collection that is the result of applying mining tools developed by MedBioinformatics solutions to the LitCovid dataset Chen et al., 2020, a very peculiar repository containing a selection of papers referring to Coronavirus 19 disease, in order to identify mentions of diseases, signs and symptoms. From this mining, the researcher were able to create two different datasets, in particular a gene evidences and a disease evidences datasets, which respectively contain a list of genes and a list of diseases.

We developed a web application that is able to perform several operations on these two data collections, for example recording the number of different disease or genes detected in literature, creating a list that display them in a sequence from A to Z.
